Welcome to Carpet Cleaning Services

We use technology in accordance with carpet washing standards and are in accordance with recommendations from the Carpet Factory. The technology used is Upright Vacuum, Dual Brush Cleaning, Carpet Extractor, and Hot Water Extraction (for stubborn stains).

Making Carpets Durable: Technology or machinery used We provide protection to the carpet and will certainly not damage carpet material. In addition to maintaining the carpet, the result is also cleaner and the carpet feathers stand back so the carpet looks like new.

Professional Team: Our carpet cleaning team is a professional and highly trained team cleaning all types of carpets. So you don't need to worry because we will make sure the carpet is washed clean and finished on time.

Free Choice of Washing Time: We give customers the freedom to choose carpet washing. But usually, to wash office carpet, done on Holidays or Saturdays & Sundays. We do not rule out the possibility of washing on weekdays (Monday - Friday).

We are a professional cleaning service intended for apartments, houses and offices, offering services including General Cleaning, Extra Cleaning, Ironing, Vacuum Mites, Mattress Washing, Sofas & Carpets and AC Services that are easily ordered through Applications, Websites and WhatsApp.

We have a vision of wanting to provide on-demand cleaning services that are easily ordered at affordable prices. Our strength compared to other competitors is that we always prioritize satisfying cleaning results and focus on room details. This is realized through cleaners who have gone through a rigorous selection process and have been given previous training.

Before becoming part of Carpet Cleaning Services, candidates must go through a rigorous selection process such as good grooming, personality tests and criminal checks. After they pass the selection, the next stage is that cleaners are required to attend training such as dealing with consumers, proper cleaning techniques and time management. Only the best candidates pass the selection and are chosen to be part of our cleaners.
